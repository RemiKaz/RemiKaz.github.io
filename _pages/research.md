## 2020: Tracking 

I started to do reserarch in my M1 internship in 2020 at the SATIE laboratory at ENS Paris-Saclay during COVID-19 pandemic. I was working in finding the best algorithm to participate to the MOT20 Pedestrian Detection Challenge, a multi-object tracking challenge on highly densely crowded scenes. I have not so much to say about this phase, honestly I was not very entousiastic working about a task that can have easily malignant applications. But honestly, considering my expertise at this time, there was very little chance that a revolutionary contribution could be made anyways. But honeslty it was a very good subject to learn the ropes. If this project did not induced a publication, the proposed method was ranked 2nd, so it was kind of a success.
